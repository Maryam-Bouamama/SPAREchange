Animals


ASPCA: The American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals, also known as the ASPCA, leads the nation in putting an end to animal abuse. Donating would help ASPCA fight against cruelty and would give animals around the world a second chance.



AWF: The African Wildlife Foundation works tirelessly to protect over 80 threatened and endangered animals in Africa. Donations are used to fund conservation programs that ensure protection for the wildlife and environments in Africa.



Health


GAIN: The Global Alliance for Improved Nutrition gives over a billion people more access to affordable and nutritious food. With their donations, they can reach even more people that face malnutrition around the world.



Red Cross: The Red Cross focuses on supporting urgent humanitarian needs and disasters big and small. Donations can go to disaster relief, where it is needed most (urgent humanitarian needs), your local Red Cross, and home fires.



Environmental


WCS: The Wildlife Conservation Society raises money and provides services to support conservation experts focus on saving wildlife. Donations can be designated towards specific species and 100% will go straight to your field of choice.



EDF: The Environmental Defense Fund takes action to rewrite the planet’s current trajectory involving climate change and the environmental state. Donations go towards the preservation of natural systems around the world.



Education


Khan Academy: Khan Academy provides free education accessible to people around the world. As a non-profit organization, Khan Academy relies on donations to be able to keep its educational program free for any user.



GWC SIP: The Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program equips girls with the opportunity to pursue careers in tech. Donations allow more girls to have access to free education in the computer science program to close the gender gap in the tech field.



Arts and Culture


Creative Capital: Creative Capital provides funding, counsel gatherings, and career development services to support artists across the country. Through donations, they are able to give artists the ability to build sustainable practices.



The Art of Elysium: The Art of Elysium Organization works to empower artists and communities to join together and share their gifts. With donations, they are able to aid the art services of community artists, giving back to the “artists who share their gifts with those in need”.



International NGO's


Givelight: The mission of Givelight is to build homes and futures for the orphans in areas affected by natural disasters, poverty, and war. Donations can provide warm meals, pay for tutors, and sponsor orphan children in need.



Unicef: Unicef supports child refugees and helps to give them the childhood that they deserve. Donations can help those desperately in need of nutrition, water, and medical supplies.
